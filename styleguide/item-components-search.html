3.2 Search

Consists out of components grouped together. They rely entirely on nesting, so make sure people are aware of this.

Example

<section class="s-block ">
    <form action="get">
        <div class="s-block-f">
            <input type="text" class="s-block-f-input text-sans w-100" name="search" id=""
                placeholder="Search...">
        </div>
    </form>
</section>
